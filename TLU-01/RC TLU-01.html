TLU-01 Module - PCB - spodekNa bottom plate is then located a pile of SMD components. The heart of the module is equipped with an integrated circuit board in a case MC34063AL DIP-8 (PTO classical case, 8 feet), which is step-up/down/inverting DC / DC converter - humanly speaking, this circuit can convert the input DC voltage necessary to higher or lower in a relatively wide range. MC34063AL converter itself has a working voltage range of 3 to 40V, output current of 1.5 A, very low power, short circuit protection on output, additional information can be found in the datasheet, which can be downloaded at the above address. The involvement of the converter circuit is also a coil, which is under the big roller round protrusion of the upper part of the box - this coil is the source of light whistling, you can hear when you turn on the module. Module TLU-01 - DC / DC converter according MC34063ALNechtělo I draw a diagram of connections, so according to the used parts I estimate that the integrated circuit produces an increased DC voltage that is used to power eight simple current sources. They are each made up of one transistor and few passive components, limited to about 20mA of current. Output current source is then connected to one of the eight connectors for lighting LEDs. I also made a few observations, because I wonder what's on LEDkových outputs and how much TLU-01 really can handle.
